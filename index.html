Upload your friends list HTML file to analyze the names.

How to Download Your Facebook Friend Data

  1. Navigate to Settings & Privacy within Facebook on the Web or app.
  2. Go to settings and find Download your Information under Your Information.
  3. Click Continue.
  4. Click Download your information.
  5. Select the profile from which you want to download your friend's list or select multiple!
  6. Then click Specific types of information. If you only want to download your friend's list, feel free to do so. It is recommended to download all the data, as it is interesting, but this will take longer.
  7. If selecting Specific types of information, scroll down and choose Friends if pulling from Facebook or Followers and Following if pulling data from Instagram.
  8. Select Download to Device and click next.
  9. Update the Date Range to All Time. Make sure the format is in HTML and the right email is selected to receive the file. Upon entering those fields, click "Create Files." Facebook will take 1-4 days (usually shorter) to process your request and provide an HTML file of your friends list to your email. If you downloaded all of your information, the friends list will be in a zip folder.
  10. Once you have the file, load it below and enjoy your findings!
